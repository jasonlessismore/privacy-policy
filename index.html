破阵蓝图 隐私政策

生效日期： 2025年8月24日

欢迎使用“破阵蓝图”（以下简称“本应用”）。我们尊重并保护您的隐私。本隐私政策将帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

我们收集的信息

为了提供和改进我们的服务，我们会收集以下类型的信息：

  • 订阅和购买信息：我们通过Apple的App Store处理您的订阅服务。为了管理您的订阅状态和恢复购买，我们会收集与您的Apple账户关联的购买历史和匿名的用户标识符。我们无法访问您的Apple ID或任何付款信息。
  • 使用历史（游戏内容）：我们会保存您在本应用中创建和分析的阵容历史。此功能旨在让您能随时回顾和查阅自己的使用记录。
  • 产品交互数据：我们会收集您如何与本应用交互的信息，例如功能使用频率、按钮点击等。这些信息是匿名的，用于帮助我们了解用户偏好，以改进产品设计和功能。
  • 诊断信息：我们会收集匿名的崩溃数据和性能数据（如应用启动时间、卡顿率）。这对于我们定位并修复潜在的技术问题、提升应用的稳定性和性能至关重要。

我们如何使用您的信息

我们使用所收集的信息主要用于以下目的：

  • 提供和维护App功能：例如，验证您的订阅状态以解锁高级功能，保存和显示您的阵容历史记录。
  • 分析和改进产品：通过分析使用和诊断数据，我们可以了解现有功能的表现，规划新功能，并持续优化应用的稳定性和用户体验。

信息共享

我们不会出售或出租您的任何个人信息。我们仅在以下情况下共享信息：

  • 服务提供商：我们使用Apple公司提供的App Store服务来处理您的订阅。您的购买行为受Apple的隐私政策约束。
  • 法律要求：在法律法规要求或响应有效的法律程序时，我们可能会披露您的信息。

数据安全

我们采取了合理的管理和技术措施来保护您的信息安全，但请您理解，没有任何网络传输或电子存储方式是100%安全的。

儿童隐私

本应用不面向13岁以下的儿童。我们不会有意收集13岁以下儿童的个人身份信息。

本隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们建议您定期查看本页面以了解任何变更。任何变更一经发布在此页面上，立即生效。

Blueprint Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 24, 2025

Welcome to "Static Blueprint" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

To provide and improve our services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Subscription and Purchase Information: We process your subscription services through Apple's App Store. To manage your subscription status and restore purchases, we collect purchase history and an anonymous user identifier associated with your Apple account. We do not have access to your Apple ID or any payment information.
  • Usage History (Gameplay Content): We save the draft analysis history you create and analyze within the App. This feature is intended to allow you to review and consult your own usage records at any time.
  • Product Interaction Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as feature usage frequency, button clicks, etc. This information is anonymous and used to help us understand user preferences to improve product design and functionality.
  • Diagnostic Information: We collect anonymous crash data and performance data (such as app launch time, hang rate). This is crucial for us to locate and fix potential technical issues and to improve the stability and performance of the App.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect primarily for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain App Functionality: For example, to verify your subscription status to unlock premium features, and to save and display your draft history.
  • For Analytics and Product Improvement: By analyzing usage and diagnostic data, we can understand the performance of existing features, plan for new features, and continuously optimize the app's stability and user experience.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent any of your personal information. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Apple's App Store service to process your subscriptions. Your purchase behavior is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to valid legal process.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect the security of your information, but please understand that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Any changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
